⚙️ How to Use This Wall

  • Self-update only — No micromanagement. You own your row.
  • Celebrate small wins — Even "✅" triggers dopamine + social proof.
  • Update daily (digital) or weekly (physical) — Sync during stand-ups.
  • Rotate wall ownership — New maintainer each week builds shared responsibility.
  • Review blockers publicly — Team swarm solves faster than solo struggle.
  • Drag, drop, done — On digital boards, move your card. On physical, move your sticky.
